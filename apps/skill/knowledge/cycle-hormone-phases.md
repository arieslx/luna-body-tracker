A practical cycle-aware model can distinguish lower-hormone days around menstruation and early follicular days from higher-hormone luteal days after ovulation. Some people tolerate intensity better in lower-hormone windows.

Higher-hormone windows may call for more attention to recovery, fueling, heat tolerance, sleep, cravings, and symptom tracking. Individual records should override generic cycle assumptions.
