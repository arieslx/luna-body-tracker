Cortisol is part of normal stress physiology. It should not be framed as automatically bad. Persistent stress signals, poor sleep, under-fueling, and repeated high-intensity training can cluster with fatigue, cravings, low mood, and poor recovery.

Recommendations should avoid fear-based hormone claims. Look for record patterns such as sleep debt, missed meals, low protein, low carbohydrate availability, or too many intense days without recovery.
